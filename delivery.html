7. Delivery Issues and Returns

If you experience any issues with your delivery, please let us know within 24 hours:

Incorrect or Missing Items: Contact us immediately to report any issues, and we’ll work quickly to resolve them. Cold or Damaged Food: While we strive to deliver your food hot and fresh, if there are issues with quality, please contact us for a solution or a potential refund.

8. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: Can I schedule a delivery for a later time? A: Yes, we offer scheduled deliveries. You can select your preferred time at checkout.

Q: Do you deliver during holidays? A: Yes, we’re open on most holidays but may operate on limited hours. Check our website or contact us for holiday hours.

Q: Can I make changes to my order after it’s been placed? A: Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to make changes. We’ll do our best to accommodate, but changes may not be possible once the order is being prepared.
